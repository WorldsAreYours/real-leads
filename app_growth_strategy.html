üî• Agent Lead Generation Process
Consumer Interaction
User chats with AI assistant about buying/selling. Every conversation tracked and analyzed for intent signals.
Qualification Scoring
AI analyzes: budget mentioned, timeline urgency, location specificity, engagement level, follow-up questions.
Lead Routing
Qualified leads (score 7+/10) automatically sent to subscribed agents in that area with full conversation context.
Agent Follow-up
Agent contacts lead within 2 hours with personalized message based on chat history. Higher conversion than cold calls.
Outcome Tracking
Agent reports back: meeting scheduled, under contract, closed deal. Data feeds back into qualification model.
Model Improvement
Successful patterns identified and applied. Failed leads analyzed to improve qualification accuracy.
